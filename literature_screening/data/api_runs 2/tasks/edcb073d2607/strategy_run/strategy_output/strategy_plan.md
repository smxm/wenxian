# 检索与筛选方案

## 研究主题

Vocal techniques in Western opera aria singing

## 需求概括

To investigate and synthesize research on singing techniques employed in arias within the context of Western opera.

## 建议用于初筛的研究主题

Western opera aria vocal techniques

## 纳入标准

- Studies focusing on opera singing or vocal performance.
- Specifically related to Western opera traditions.
- Includes arias as the subject of study.
- Addresses singing techniques, vocal pedagogy, or performance practice.

## 排除标准

- Excludes non-Western opera forms (e.g., Chinese opera, if not comparative).
- Excludes instrumental music, composition, or non-singing aspects.
- Excludes general music education not specific to opera.
- Excludes popular music or non-classical vocal techniques.

## 检索词与高级检索式

### Scopus advanced search for Western opera aria singing techniques

```text
TITLE-ABS-KEY( ( opera OR "Western opera" ) AND ( aria OR arias ) AND ( "singing technique*" OR "vocal technique*" OR "vocal pedagogy" OR "performance practice" ) )
```

- 说明：Use this query in Scopus advanced search interface.
- 说明：Adjust date range or language filters as needed.
