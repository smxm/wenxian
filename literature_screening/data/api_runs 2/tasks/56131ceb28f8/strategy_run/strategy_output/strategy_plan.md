# 检索与筛选方案

## 研究主题

IP image design for travel agencies incorporating research travel and architectural visual elements

## 需求概括

The user seeks Chinese literature from 2020 onwards on designing IP images for travel agencies, specifically Nanjing Fangcao International Travel Agency, with a focus on research travel features, integration of architecture, and aspects of illustration and poster design.

## 建议用于初筛的研究主题

IP design travel agency research travel architecture illustration

## 纳入标准

- Studies published in or after 2020.
- Primary topic is IP image or brand identity design in the travel or tourism sector.
- Includes elements of research travel, educational tourism, or study tours.
- Discusses integration with architecture, spatial design, or visual arts like illustration and poster design.
- Written in Chinese language.

## 排除标准

- Studies not related to travel, tourism, or hospitality.
- No focus on design, branding, or IP development.
- Published before 2020.
- In languages other than Chinese.
- Focused solely on marketing or management without design aspects.

## 检索词与高级检索式

### Scopus advanced search for IP design in Chinese travel agencies

```text
TITLE-ABS-KEY( "IP形象" OR "品牌设计" OR "IP design" ) AND TITLE-ABS-KEY( "旅行社" OR "旅游机构" OR "travel agency" ) AND TITLE-ABS-KEY( "研学" OR "教育旅游" OR "research travel" ) AND TITLE-ABS-KEY( "建筑" OR "architecture" OR "插画" OR "illustration" OR "海报" OR "poster" ) AND PUBYEAR > 2019 AND LANGUAGE( chinese )
```

- 说明：Adjust search terms based on initial results; use TITLE-ABS-KEY for comprehensive coverage.
- 说明：Consider refining with specific terms like "芳草国际旅行社" if noise is high.

### Web of Science advanced search for IP design in Chinese travel agencies

```text
TS=( "IP形象" OR "品牌设计" ) AND TS=( "旅行社" OR "旅游机构" ) AND TS=( "研学" OR "教育旅游" ) AND TS=( "建筑" OR "插画" OR "海报" ) AND PY=2020-2024 AND LA=chinese
```

- 说明：Use TS field for topic search covering title, abstract, and keywords.
- 说明：Update PY range as needed for current year; consider adding more visual design terms.

## 检索边界与注意事项

- The search may retrieve studies not specific to travel agencies; manually screen for relevance.
- Some relevant studies might be in English; if results are limited, consider removing language filter.
- Architecture and illustration terms could introduce noise; refine with additional context terms during screening.
